Who is This Guy Anyway?

Elliott burst onto the voiceover scene more than a decade ago, and ever since, the voiceover scene has been……well, pretty much the same, but at least it's keeping Elliott busy.  His booming baritone has been heard literally thousands of times on TV, radio, the Web, and plenty of other places.

Several years back, if you were anywhere near a TV, chances are you heard Elliott exhorting you to “pick your sport, pick your players, and pick up your cash.”  Yep, he was the voice of those unrelenting DraftKings commercials that blanketed the airwaves all through that fateful summer.

Nowadays, Elliott works out of his professional-quality home studio, equipped with SourceConnect, Zoom, phone patch, his Sennheiser 416 mic, and anything else you might need to bring your project to life.  Reach out to him directly or through one of his agents to book him for your next project.
